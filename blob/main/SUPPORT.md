## FAQ

**Q: How do I undo a move?**
A: Tap the "Undo" button (curved arrow) at the bottom left of the screen.

**Q: Can I play with a friend?**
A: Yes! The game supports two-player mode on the same watch. Just take turns placing pieces.

**Q: How do I zoom?**
A: Use the Digital Crown to zoom in and out of the board.
