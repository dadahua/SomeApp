# Support for 2048 Watch

Welcome to the support page for 2048 Watch, the classic 2048 puzzle game optimized for your wrist!

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### Q1: How do I play the game?
*   **Swipe**: Swipe your finger on the Apple Watch screen to move the tiles.
*   **Merge**: When two tiles with the same number touch, they merge into one.
*   **Goal**: Try to create a tile with the number 2048!

### Q2: The game is not responding to swipes.
*   **Restart**: Try force-quitting the app and reopening it. On Apple Watch, press the side button, swipe left on the app card, and tap the delete icon.
*   **Update**: Ensure your Apple Watch is running the latest version of watchOS.

### Q3: How do I change the language?
*   The game automatically detects your Apple Watch's system language settings.
*   To change the language, go to your iPhone's Settings -> General -> Language & Region, change the language, and the app will reflect the change upon restart.
*   Supported languages include: English, Simplified Chinese, [List other languages here].

### Q4: Did you remove my high score?
*   Scores are stored locally on your device. If you delete the app or unpair your watch, the data will be lost.
*   [**OPTIONAL**] If iCloud sync is implemented: Scores are synced via iCloud. Ensure you are logged into the same Apple ID and have iCloud enabled for the app.

### Q5: Is there an iPhone version?
*   Currently, 2048 Watch is a standalone Apple Watch application. There is no companion iPhone app required to play.
